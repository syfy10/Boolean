Boolean Email Recipes demo script

1. Boolean connects Gmail or Outlook once using provider OAuth.
2. Mailbox access stays on the user's PC. Boolean cloud does not store Gmail or Outlook tokens.
3. Boolean asks before sending email or changing mailbox contents.
4. Cleanup recipes preview counts and sample messages before action.
5. Important mail is protected: starred, labeled, attachment, legal, financial, personal, and receipt-like messages are excluded.
6. Cleanup moves approved messages to Trash first, not permanent delete.
7. Undo remains available while messages are in Trash.
